Settings for Me:

Welcome to your Support Network. This network is an automatic system that informs the three people in your network in the case that data analysis yields results indicating you have not been feeling well lately. We know it's hard to ask people for help and support. We also know that sometimes, you yourself don't know that you could use someone's help or support. My Support Network takes care of updating, informing and explaining your feelings to the people in your network.

Click on individual members of your Support Network to view detailed information about their profile and to manage their settings.

Click on "Manage My Support Network" to edit members of your network and their contact information.

Use your mouse pad to zoom in and out of the network.
